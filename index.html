Stage 1
Consultation and Requirement Discussion

The first stage involves establishing contact with the renovation company. The client meets with company representatives to discuss their needs, desires, and requirements regarding the renovation. At this stage, it's essential to determine the scope of work, budget, timelines, and other key project aspects.

Stage 2
Design Development

After the consultation, the company develops a design project that meets the client's needs and budget. This stage includes finalizing planning, selecting materials, color schemes, and other details necessary for the renovation.

Stage 3
Contract Signing

After the design approval and agreement on all project details, the client and the company sign a contract for the renovation services. This contract documents all the terms of collaboration, including the scope of work, cost, timelines, and other conditions.

Stage 4
Execution of Renovation Works

Upon contract signing, the company's team of professionals begins executing the renovation works according to the agreed plan. During the works, the company ensures the quality and compliance of all works with safety standards and building regulations.

Stage 5
Completion and Project Handover

After the completion of renovation works, the client and company representatives conduct a final inspection and acceptance of the works. If the client is satisfied with the results, the project is considered complete, and the company hands over the keys to the newly renovated or updated apartment or house to the client.
